  • Execute all visual design stages of website design from concept to final hand-off to development.

  • Update & keep all agency collateral materials, including keeping records of Client's logos, fonts, images, etc.

Who can apply
  • Bachelor's Degree.

  • HTML, CSS and JavaScript experience a plus.
